What is GreenGate? ‚ñº

GreenGate is an automated geospatial validation platform that cross-references rural property coordinates with 6 official Brazilian environmental data layers (PRODES, FUNAI, IBAMA, ICMBio, INCRA, Legal Amazon). We generate auditable PDF reports in under 2 minutes, with a 0-100 risk score and QR Code for public verification.

Which data layers do you validate? ‚ñº

6 official layers:

  • PRODES (INPE) - Deforestation in Legal Amazon
  • Indigenous Lands (FUNAI) - Homologated demarcations
  • Conservation Units (ICMBio) - Protected areas
  • IBAMA Embargoes - Environmental restrictions
  • Quilombola Territories (INCRA) - Traditional communities
  • Legal Amazon - Special legislation area
How does the 0-100 risk score work? ‚ñº

Our proprietary algorithm analyzes:

  • Overlaps: Total area overlapping with each layer
  • Severity: Differentiated weights (e.g., PRODES has higher weight than Legal Amazon)
  • Multiple violations: Progressive penalization

Classification: 90-100 (Low), 70-89 (Medium), 50-69 (High), 0-49 (Critical)

How long does a validation take? ‚ñº

Under 2 minutes from geometry submission to complete PDF generation. Processing is done via optimized PostGIS in PostgreSQL database.

Is the report valid for EUDR compliance? ‚ñº

Which geometry formats are accepted? ‚ñº

GeoJSON with types:

  • Polygon
  • MultiPolygon

Coordinate system: WGS84 (EPSG:4326)

Geometry constraints:

  • Maximum vertices: 10,000
  • Maximum area: 10,000 hectares
  • Coordinates must be within Brazil bounding box

We automatically validate and correct invalid geometries (self-intersections, holes, etc).
